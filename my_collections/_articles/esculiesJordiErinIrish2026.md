  \ used a patriotic play, Jordi Er\xEDn, set in Ireland and focused on Daniel O\u2019\

  \ the demand for autonomy in Catalonia in the first quarter of the twentieth century.\
  \ The research is a case study of political and cultural transfers between nationalisms.\

  \ World War. On the first occasion, nationalists used it to ask for unity within\
  \ the Catalanist movement and, on the second, to oppose the civil government of\
