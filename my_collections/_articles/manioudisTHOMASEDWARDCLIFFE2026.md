abstract: "Thomas Edward Cliffe Leslie is an influential figure in the Irish history\
  \ of economic thought. This paper presents Leslie\u2019s critical appraisal of British\
  \ land systems and discusses his critique of the classical landed property reform.\
  \ The paper illustrates the affinities between Leslie\u2019s and John Stuart Mill\u2019\
  s economic thought regarding the Irish land question. Furthermore, it shows that\
  \ Leslie\u2019s support for small proprietorship in Ireland is regarded as a prerequisite\
  \ for defending the Act of Union of 1800. Lastly, the paper argues that Leslie\u2019\
  s political economy of British land systems is a significant stream in the foundation\
  \ of the Irish historical school and in the promotion of an alternative political\
  \ economy."
